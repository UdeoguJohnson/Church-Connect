Basic Features:

Church Registration:
Churches sign up with unique church name (username) and password.
Passwords stored hashed for security.
Member Registration / Login:
Members create accounts and search/join a church by its name.
When joining, their record is linked to the church.
Authentication:
Use Django built in Authentication.
Church Admin Panel:
Once logged in, churches can post/manage events, view members, view prayer request.
